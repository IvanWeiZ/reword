FULL DESCRIPTION (Chrome Web Store):

Stop sending messages you'll regret.

We've all been there. You're frustrated, you type something sharp, and you hit Send. Five minutes later, you wish you hadn't. Reword catches those moments before they happen.

HOW IT WORKS

Reword watches as you compose messages in Gmail, LinkedIn, and Twitter/X. When it detects passive-aggression, dismissiveness, or harsh tone, a subtle "Review tone" badge appears near the Send button. Click it to see three AI-powered rewrites:

  - Warmer — empathetic and friendly
  - Direct but kind — clear without the edge
  - Minimal change — smallest possible fix

Pick one with a single click, or send your original. You're always in control.

SMART THREE-TIER AI

Most messages are checked instantly with a local heuristic — no network call, no delay. Only flagged messages go to AI:

  1. Local keyword and pattern analysis (under 5ms)
  2. Chrome's built-in on-device AI (free, no API key needed)
  3. Gemini 2.5 Flash for nuanced cases (uses your own API key)

This means most of your writing never leaves your browser.

RELATIONSHIP-AWARE REWRITES

Configure relationship contexts per platform — workplace, romantic, or family. Reword adjusts its suggestions based on who you're talking to. A message to your partner gets different rewrites than one to your manager.
